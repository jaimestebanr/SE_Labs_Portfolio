This activity diagram represents the workflow of a Car Insurance System, showing the steps involved in purchasing an insurance policy.

The process begins with searching and selecting a policy, followed by entering customer details and proceeding to checkout. A decision node is used to determine the payment method, such as cash or credit card, leading to different processing paths.

After a successful payment, the system generates the policy document and confirms the order. Parallel activities are included, such as issuing the insurance policy and sending a confirmation email, which are later synchronized.

The diagram also models alternative flows, such as application cancellation at different stages of the process.

Overall, it illustrates the sequence of activities and decision points involved in completing an insurance application.
