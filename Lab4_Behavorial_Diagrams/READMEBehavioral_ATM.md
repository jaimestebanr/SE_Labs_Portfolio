This sequence diagram represents the interaction between different components of an ATM (Automated Teller Machine) system during a typical transaction.

The diagram shows how the Customer, ATM, Bank Server, and Bank Account communicate through a sequence of messages. It includes steps such as inserting the card, verifying the card and PIN, entering the withdrawal amount, and processing the transaction.

Alternative flows are also represented, such as invalid card, incorrect PIN, and insufficient funds, showing how the system handles errors and different scenarios.

Overall, the diagram models the dynamic behavior of the ATM system, illustrating how information flows between components to complete a transaction.
