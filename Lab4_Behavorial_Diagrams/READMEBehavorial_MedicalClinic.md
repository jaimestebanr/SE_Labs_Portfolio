This state diagram represents the different states of a Medical Clinic System during its operation.

The diagram shows how the system transitions between states such as Off, Self-Diagnostics, Idle, Operational, Out of Service, and Maintenance. It begins with system startup, followed by diagnostic checks, and then moves to an idle state where it waits for user interaction.

The Operational state is modeled as a composite state, including internal processes such as patient authentication, appointment handling, and consultation or payment.

The diagram also includes alternative transitions, such as system errors leading to an out-of-service state and maintenance, as well as successful service completion returning the system to idle.

Overall, it illustrates the lifecycle and behavior of the system under different conditions.
