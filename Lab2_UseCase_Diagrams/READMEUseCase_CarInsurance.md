This use case diagram represents a Car Insurance System, showing the interactions between the main actors and the system functionalities.

The diagram includes two actors: Client and Claims Officer. The Client can perform actions such as requesting a coverage estimate, purchasing an insurance plan, filing an insurance claim, and checking the progress of a claim.

The Claims Officer is responsible for evaluating and processing claims submitted by clients. The diagram also shows relationships between use cases, such as including validation of driver details during the purchase process and extending claim evaluation from claim tracking.

Overall, the diagram provides a clear overview of the system’s functionalities and how users interact with it.
