This use case diagram represents a Medical Clinic System and the interactions between its main actors.

The system includes two actors: Patient and Receptionist. The Patient can book appointments, view appointment details, update personal data, and cancel appointments. The Receptionist is responsible for registering patients and printing appointment slips.

Several operations include verification of patient information to ensure correct and secure data management. Overall, the diagram provides a clear overview of the system’s functionalities and user interactions.
