This activity diagram represents the workflow of a Gym Management System during the class booking process.

The diagram shows the sequence of activities starting from user login and validation, followed by viewing available classes, selecting a class, and checking membership status and availability. Decision nodes are used to handle different conditions, such as invalid credentials, inactive membership, or full classes.

If all conditions are satisfied, the system proceeds to create the booking, update records, and send a confirmation message. Alternative paths are included for errors and cancellation scenarios.

Overall, the diagram illustrates the flow of actions and decision points involved in completing a booking within the system.
